1 I will give You thanks with all my heart; I will sing Your praises before the gods.
2 I will bow down toward Your holy temple And give thanks to Your name for Your mercy and Your truth; For You have made Your word great according to all Your name.
3 On the day I called, You answered me; You made me bold with strength in my soul.
4 ¶All the kings of the earth will give thanks to You, Lord, When they have heard the words of Your mouth.
5 And they will sing of the ways of the Lord, For great is the glory of the Lord.
6 For the Lord is exalted, Yet He looks after the lowly, But He knows the haughty from afar.
7 ¶Though I walk in the midst of trouble, You will revive me; You will reach out with Your hand against the wrath of my enemies, And Your right hand will save me.
8 The Lord will accomplish what concerns me; Your faithfulness, Lord, is everlasting; Do not abandon the works of Your hands.
