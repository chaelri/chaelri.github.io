How it works

  1. Paste your discussion questions exactly as received.
  2. Tap Generate — AI picks emphasis (bold + italic) without changing your wording.
  3. Preview the rendered image; Reformat for a different take.
  4. Copy to clipboard, download, or upload to Google Drive (returns a shareable link).

First Drive upload signs you in with Google. Files save to your own Drive in a folder called SNS Discussion Questions.
